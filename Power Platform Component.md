## The Power Platform Component:

# A Model-Driven App is the right choice for this solution for three reasons. First, the system is fundamentally data-centric — it is built around structured records (tickets, agents, SLAs) with well-defined relationships and rules, which is exactly what Model-Driven Apps are optimised for. Second, the app needs to serve multiple user roles (requesters, agents, managers) with different views and permissions, which the Model-Driven App’s role-based security model handles natively. Third, the organisation needs audit trails, automated workflows, and reporting out of the box — all of which come built into Dataverse and the Power Platform without additional development.
